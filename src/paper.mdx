## Abstract

We present OmniBody, a real-time soft-body simulation framework that achieves global deformation through purely local, voxel aligned oriented ellipsoidal particle interactions. A suite of decoupled constraints — contact, bending, anisotropic elasticity, volume preservation, and distance-based fracture — operates over a six neighbor structure, enabling GPU parallelization without global solvers or remeshing.
